1. Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

✅ Reduce waste – Avoid single-use plastics (bags, straws, bottles) and opt for reusable alternatives.
✅ Reuse items – Repurpose containers, donate old clothes, and buy second-hand goods.
✅ Recycle properly – Separate paper, glass, plastic, and metals to reduce landfill waste.
2. Conserve Water

💧 Fix leaks promptly.
💧 Take shorter showers and turn off taps when not in use.
💧 Use water-efficient appliances and collect rainwater for gardening.
3. Save Energy & Switch to Renewables

🔌 Turn off lights and unplug devices when not in use.
☀️ Use energy-efficient LED bulbs and appliances.
⚡ Support renewable energy (solar, wind, hydro) where possible.
4. Adopt Sustainable Eating Habits

🌱 Eat more plant-based meals (reducing meat lowers carbon footprint).
🍎 Buy local, seasonal, and organic produce to cut down on transport emissions.
🚫 Minimize food waste by planning meals and composting scraps.
5. Protect Wildlife & Ecosystems

🐝 Plant native trees and flowers to support pollinators.
🚯 Avoid products linked to deforestation (like palm oil if unsustainable).
🏞️ Support conservation efforts and avoid disturbing natural habitats.
6. Reduce Carbon Footprint

🚶 Walk, bike, or use public transport instead of driving.
✈️ Limit air travel when possible; opt for trains or video calls.
🏠 Improve home insulation to reduce heating/cooling needs.
7. Advocate & Educate

📢 Support environmental policies and vote for eco-conscious leaders.
📚 Educate friends and family on sustainability.
🤝 Join or donate to environmental organizations (e.g., WWF, Greenpeace).
8. Go Digital & Reduce Paper Use

📧 Opt for e-bills and digital documents instead of printing.
📖 Use e-books and online subscriptions to cut paper waste.
9. Support Ethical & Eco-Friendly Brands

🛍️ Choose companies with sustainable practices (fair trade, carbon-neutral, etc.).
👚 Buy less fast fashion—opt for quality, long-lasting clothing.
10. Be Mindful of Pollution

🚭 Avoid smoking and littering.
🚗 Maintain your vehicle to reduce emissions.
🧴Use eco-friendly cleaning and personal care products.
